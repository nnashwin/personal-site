Incremental Learning

During my school years I forgot how to learn. Whether it was due to my teachers or the system itself, I lost the ability to teach myself.

Following this loss, my love for life also started to decrease. A feeling of hopelessness crawled into the back of my head. In high school, I spent many a plan-less Friday night grappling with a feeling of anxiety. It felt like the whole world was passing me by.
